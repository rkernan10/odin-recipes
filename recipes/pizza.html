Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe is for the most delicious NY style pizza. You are sure to enjoy.

One recipe feeds four, double for a party.

Ingredients

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Dough

Steps

  1. Roll dough
  2. Spread marinara
  3. Spread cheese
  4. Spread pepperoni
  5. Bake
